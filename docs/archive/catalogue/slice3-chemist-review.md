# Archived Slice 3 review handoff

This document formerly described the lithium-only external review gate. That
trust root was first superseded by the generalized alkali-water catalogue and
is now superseded by the five-family aggregate in
`catalogue/trusted/core-chemistry/`.

The current source-controlled trust decision consists of:

- the five deterministic candidate packages under `catalogue/candidates/`;
- the separate host-selected AI attestation under `catalogue/reviews/`;
- the generated catalogue, review, and promotion manifest under
  `catalogue/trusted/`; and
- the catalogue and review semantic digests compiled into `TrustedCatalogue`.

The current attestation explicitly states that AI review can be wrong and is
not human expert certification. It covers 118 element identity records and 36
supported finite bindings across five generalized families. Candidate packages
and runtime agents cannot extend the compiled trust root.
